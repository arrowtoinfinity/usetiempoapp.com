FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data private and secure?

+

Yes. Your data stays on your device and is synced securely through iCloud. We don't have access to your personal information, calendar events, or health data. Everything is encrypted and only accessible by you.

What devices does Nickel work on?

+

Nickel is available on iPhone and iPad running iOS 16 or later. Your data syncs seamlessly across all your Apple devices using iCloud.

Does Nickel work offline?

+

Yes! All core features work offline. You can view your schedule, add events, track habits, and use the AI planner without an internet connection. Changes sync automatically when you're back online.

How does the AI planning assistant work?

+

Our AI analyzes your calendar, habits, and goals to provide personalized recommendations. It suggests optimal times for tasks, identifies scheduling conflicts, and helps you maintain work-life balance. You're always in control—AI suggestions are just recommendations.

Can I sync with Google Calendar or Outlook?

+

Yes! Nickel integrates with Google Calendar, Outlook, and Apple Calendar. All your events stay in sync across platforms.

What's included in the free version?

+

The free version includes basic calendar features, habit tracking, and daily reminders. Premium features like AI planning, nutrition scanning, and advanced analytics require a subscription.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

+

Yes, you can cancel anytime through your App Store subscription settings. Your premium features will remain active until the end of your billing period.
