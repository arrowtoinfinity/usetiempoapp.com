Common Topics

Calendar Sync Issues
If your calendars aren't syncing, try disconnecting and reconnecting your calendar account in Settings. Make sure Tiempo has permission to access your calendar.

Subscription & Billing
Subscriptions are managed through the App Store. To cancel or modify your subscription, go to Settings on your iPhone > Apple ID > Subscriptions > Tiempo.

Feature Requests
We love hearing your ideas! Send feature requests to feedback@usetiempoapp.com

System Requirements

Tiempo requires iOS 17.0 or later. For the best experience, we recommend keeping your device updated to the latest iOS version.
